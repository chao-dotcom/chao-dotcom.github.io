# Profile Photo

Please add your profile photo as:
- File name: profile-photo.jpg
- Location: assets/images/profile-photo.jpg
- Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square aspect ratio)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- The image will be automatically cropped to a circle

## Image Requirements
- High quality, professional headshot
- Good lighting and clear background
- Square aspect ratio works best
- File size: Keep under 2MB for web optimization
